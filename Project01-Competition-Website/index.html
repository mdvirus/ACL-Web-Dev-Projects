Book Reading Competition 2024

Event Details

Once again, AUSH Cyber Library and Dreams for Tomorrow are arranging their signature event Annual Book Reading Competition this year. This year's theme is nature. Any high school going students of Haragach Paurasava can participate in the competition.

Category:

  1. Junior (Class 6-8)
  2. Senior (Class 9-10)

Books to read:

  • The Invention of Nature by Andrea Wulf
  • The Forest Unseen by David Haskell
  • My Life with the Chimpanzees by Jane Goodall
  • The Hidden Life of Trees by Peter Wohlleben

Prizes:

  • 1st Place: Medal, Certificate, Books, and BDT1000.
  • 2nd Place: Medal, Certificate, and Books
  • 3rd Place: Medal and Certificate
